# Super Simple Steps to See Your Game

## Easiest Way: CodeSandbox (5 minutes)

1. Open: https://codesandbox.io
2. Click: "Create" → "React"
3. Delete everything in the left panel
4. Copy these 3 files from your computer:
   - `src/App.js` 
   - `src/index.js`
   - `package.json`
5. Paste them into CodeSandbox (same folder names)
6. Upload your board image: `public/torahpoly_board.png`
7. Done! Game appears on the right side

That's it! No GitHub, no Vercel, no commands needed.

---

## If You Want a Live URL (Vercel):

1. Go to vercel.com
2. Click "Add New Project"
3. Connect your GitHub account
4. Select your repository
5. Click "Deploy"
6. Done! You get a URL like: yourgame.vercel.app

The game will update automatically when you push to GitHub.
